Welcome to the World of Honey Bees

Honey bees are incredible insects that play a vital role in our ecosystem. They are known for producing honey and pollinating plants.

Interesting Facts

  • Honey bees live in colonies with a queen, workers, and drones.
  • A single bee can visit up to 5,000 flowers in a day.
  • Honey bees communicate through dances to tell others where food is.
  • They produce honey as a food source for the winter.

Why Bees Matter

Bees are essential for pollinating many of the fruits and vegetables we eat. Without them, our food supply would be greatly affected.
